About Us


Two girls on a mission to create something fancy for the people of their land. Greatly inspired by the traditional Italian pasta, introducing first time in Pakistan, the rich element of Italy - La Pasta.

We truly believe the choices we make about what we eat, where it comes from and how it's prepared have a direct and powerful impact on health of every individual, communities and the environment.

A Message from the Founders


Frederico Fellini said, "Life is a combination of magic and pasta." We couldn't agree more. Our love for pasta made us take such a huge step at such a young age. We hope you enjoy the food and have a good time.
